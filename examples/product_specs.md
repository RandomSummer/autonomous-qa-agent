# E-Shop Product Specifications

## Product Catalog
- Item 1: Laptop - $999
- Item 2: Mouse - $29
- Item 3: Keyboard - $79

## Discount Code System
- **SAVE15**: Applies 15% discount on total order
- **SAVE20**: Applies 20% discount on total order
- **NEWUSER10**: Applies 10% discount for first-time users
- Invalid codes should display: "Invalid discount code"
- Discount codes are case-sensitive

## Shipping Methods
- **Standard Shipping**: Free (5-7 business days)
- **Express Shipping**: $10 (1-2 business days)
- Default selection should be Standard

## Payment Methods
- **Credit Card**: Requires card details
- **PayPal**: Redirects to PayPal gateway
- At least one payment method must be selected

## Order Processing
- Minimum order amount: $10
- Maximum order amount: $10,000
- Orders under minimum should show error
- Successful payment displays: "Payment Successful!"
